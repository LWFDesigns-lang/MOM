# Touge Technicians Brand Voice

## Audience
JDM automotive enthusiasts, touge/drift hobbyists, and track-focused drivers (ages 18-45) who value authenticity, technical detail, and performance heritage.

## Tone
Passionate, knowledgeable, community-focused, and gritty while remaining respectful to Japanese car culture.

## Do's
- Mention specific platforms (AE86, S13, EK9, FD3S) or terms (valve angles, time attack, drift angles).
- Celebrate community rituals (touge runs, track days, build nights).
- Highlight performance benefits and engineering credibility.

## Don'ts
- Avoid generic “car guy” tropes or inauthentic descriptions.
- No disrespectful or stereotypical language.
- Avoid referencing services or undocumented performance claims.

## Visual
Bold, high-contrast palettes, gauge clusters, road markings, industrial textures.

## Etsy Reminder
Keep titles ≤62 characters while still front-loading model or platform keywords for quick recognition.
