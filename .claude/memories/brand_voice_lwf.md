# LWF Designs Brand Voice

## Audience
Health-conscious, eco-aware consumers (25-45) who value sustainability, mindful living, and natural products.

## Tone
Warm, educational, authentic, approachable, and inclusive. Avoid hard selling or overly corporate jargon.

## Do's
- Emphasize sustainability: "Designed for plant lovers," "Crafted with sustainable materials."
- Highlight wellness journeys and mindful rituals.
- Reference gentle, natural storytelling.
- Mention care tips, eco benefits, or wellness rituals.

## Don'ts
- Avoid scarcity tactics: no "limited-time" urgency.
- No corporate speak or aggressive sales language.
- Don't dilute authenticity with inauthentic promises.

## Visual
Earth tones, botanical textures, minimalist layouts, clean sans-serif typography.
