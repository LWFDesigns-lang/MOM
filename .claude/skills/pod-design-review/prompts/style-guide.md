# LWF Design Style Guide

## Brand Overview

**Extracted from:** CLAUDE_CODE_2.0_POD_BUSINESS_SETUP_GUIDE.md (lines 596-639)

LWF Designs (Living With Freedom) is a POD brand targeting lifestyle-conscious consumers with uplifting, positive, and inclusive messaging. The brand operates in the mid-range price point ($25-45) and focuses on clean, modern, minimalist aesthetics.

**Target Audience:** Broad appeal, lifestyle-conscious consumers  
**Price Point:** Mid-range ($25-45)  
**Tone:** Uplifting, positive, inclusive

## Color Palette

### Primary Colors
- **Pastels:** Soft, calming tones
- **Neutrals:** Cream, beige, soft gray
- **Earth Tones:** Greens, browns, natural hues

### Specific Color References
- `#A8D8A8` - Pastel green (plants, nature)
- `#F5E6D3` - Warm neutral (backgrounds)
- `#E8F5E9` - Light mint (accents)

### Color Usage Rules
- High contrast for readability
- Avoid neon colors (reproduction issues)
- Transparent or subtle texture backgrounds only
- Prefer pastels and earth tones over bold primaries

## Typography Guidelines

### Font Characteristics
- **Style:** Sans-serif, modern, clean
- **Readability:** Must be readable from 10 feet
- **Text Hierarchy:** Main message bold, 60%+ of design height

### Text Content
- Keep text elements <5 words for maximum impact
- Use uplifting, positive messaging
- Inspirational quotes in simple, direct language
- No political or religious statements

## Composition Rules

### Layout Principles
- **Simplicity:** Simple enough to read from 10 feet
- **Minimalism:** Clean, uncluttered designs
- **Focus:** Main message should dominate the composition
- **Backgrounds:** Transparent or subtle textures only
- **Spacing:** Generous whitespace for breathing room

### Design Complexity
- Avoid overly busy/complex designs
- Line art and minimal illustrations preferred
- Watercolor styles acceptable if not too detailed

## Motif Preferences

### Primary Themes
1. **Lifestyle & Wellness**
   - Yoga, meditation, mindfulness
   - Self-care, personal growth
   - Work-life balance

2. **Nature & Outdoor**
   - Plants, botanical elements
   - Landscapes, natural scenes
   - Eco-friendly messaging

3. **Inspirational Messaging**
   - Positive affirmations
   - Motivational quotes
   - Freedom, growth themes

4. **Pet Lovers**
   - Dogs, cats (realistic or minimalist styles)
   - Pet care, pet parent themes

5. **Hobbies**
   - Gardening, reading, yoga
   - Crafts, creative pursuits

### Preferred Visual Styles
- Watercolor botanical illustrations
- Line art (continuous line, minimalist)
- Vintage-inspired but clean
- Geometric patterns (simple)

## Elements to Avoid

### Content Restrictions
- ❌ Political statements
- ❌ Religious symbols
- ❌ Controversial topics
- ❌ Negative messaging
- ❌ Copyrighted logos/brands

### Visual Restrictions
- ❌ Overly busy/complex designs
- ❌ Neon colors (poor reproduction)
- ❌ Gradients with >3 colors
- ❌ Heavy textures or backgrounds
- ❌ Small text (<12pt effective size)

## Product Categories

### Primary Products
1. **Apparel:** T-shirts, hoodies, tank tops
2. **Home Decor:** Wall art, throw pillows, mugs
3. **Accessories:** Tote bags, phone cases

### Design Considerations by Product
- **T-shirts:** Centered chest designs, 12"x12" max
- **Hoodies:** Consider pocket placement
- **Mugs:** Wrap-around or single-side designs
- **Tote bags:** Vertical orientation works best

## Example Descriptions

### On-Brand Design Example

**"Botanical Bliss" T-Shirt**
- Watercolor potted plants in pastel greens and earth tones
- Minimalist line art style
- Text: "Plant Parent Life" in clean sans-serif
- Colors: `#A8D8A8`, `#F5E6D3`, `#E8F5E9`
- Isolated white background
- Simple, readable from distance
- Uplifting, positive message

**AI Prompt Example:**
```
Watercolor potted plants, pastel green palette, minimalist line art, 
clean sans-serif text 'Plant Parent Life', isolated white background, 
t-shirt design, high contrast
```

### Off-Brand Design Example

**What to Avoid:**
- Political slogan shirts (controversial content)
- Neon colors on black background (reproduction issues)
- Complex photorealistic designs (too busy)
- Religious symbols or messaging (not inclusive)
- Tiny detailed text (not readable)
- Heavy grunge textures (too cluttered)

## Brand Voice Characteristics

**Communication Style:**
- **Tone:** Warm, encouraging, supportive
- **Language:** Simple, accessible, positive
- **Approach:** Inclusive, non-judgmental, uplifting
- **Personality:** Friendly, optimistic, genuine

**Message Examples:**
- ✅ "Choose Joy" - Simple, positive
- ✅ "Plant Parent Life" - Relatable, fun
- ✅ "Find Your Freedom" - Empowering, brand-aligned
- ❌ "I Hate Mondays" - Negative
- ❌ "Make America Great" - Political
- ❌ "Jesus Saves" - Religious

## Quality Checklist

Before finalizing any design:
- [ ] Readable from 10 feet away
- [ ] High contrast colors (print-safe)
- [ ] Text elements <5 words
- [ ] No copyrighted content
- [ ] Culturally appropriate
- [ ] Positive/uplifting message
- [ ] Print-safe colors (no neon, limited gradients)
- [ ] Aligns with brand values (freedom, positivity, inclusivity)
- [ ] Suitable for target audience
- [ ] Appropriate for product type

# Touge Technicians Design Style Guide

## Brand Overview

**Extracted from:** CLAUDE_CODE_2.0_POD_BUSINESS_SETUP_GUIDE.md (lines 641-687)

Touge Technicians is a premium POD brand ($35-55) targeting mountain automotive enthusiasts, JDM culture fans, and drift/rally enthusiasts. The brand emphasizes technical precision, Japanese-inspired design, and authentic motorsport heritage.

**Target Audience:** Mountain automotive enthusiasts, JDM culture, drift/rally fans  
**Price Point:** Premium ($35-55)  
**Tone:** Passionate, technical, community-driven

## Color Palette

### Primary Colors
- **Bold Primaries:** Red, white, black
- **Racing Colors:** Bold, high-contrast schemes
- **Japanese Aesthetic:** Traditional color combinations

### Color Usage Rules
- High contrast essential (avoid pastels on white)
- Bold red accents signature to brand
- Black/white base with color accents
- Racing-inspired color schemes

## Typography Guidelines

### Font Characteristics
- **Style:** Angular, racing-inspired fonts
- **Aesthetic:** Technical, Japanese-influenced
- **Readability:** Bold, strong letterforms
- **Hierarchy:** Technical precision in layout

### Text Content
- Authentic Japanese terminology encouraged
- Technical automotive terms (when accurate)
- Bilingual elements (Japanese + English)
- Racing/motorsport references

## Composition Rules

### Layout Principles
- **Dynamic:** Implied motion and speed
- **Technical:** Blueprint/schematic aesthetics acceptable
- **Angular:** Sharp, precise line work
- **Authentic:** True to JDM heritage

### Design Elements
- Drift lines, tire marks
- Technical diagrams/cutaways
- Mountain pass topography
- Japanese kanji/katakana accents

## Motif Preferences

### Primary Themes
1. **Mountain Passes (Touge)**
   - Hakone, Akina, Irohazaka routes
   - Elevation maps, topographic elements
   - Hairpin turns, switchbacks

2. **JDM Car Models**
   - AE86, S13, RX-7, GT-R, Supra
   - Authentic specifications
   - Technical cutaway views

3. **Drift & Rally Culture**
   - Drift techniques and aesthetics
   - Rally events and locations
   - Motorsport heritage

4. **Technical Precision**
   - Blueprint/schematic style
   - Japanese + English technical terms
   - Authentic automotive details

### Preferred Visual Styles
- Clean vector illustrations
- Technical blueprint aesthetics
- Dynamic angle compositions
- Japanese-inspired line art
- Motion-implied designs

## Elements to Avoid

### Content Restrictions
- ❌ Generic "JDM" without context
- ❌ Copyrighted logos/brands (manufacturer marks)
- ❌ Overuse of "rising sun" motif (stereotypical)
- ❌ Fast & Furious movie references (not authentic)
- ❌ Anime clichés/stereotypes

### Cultural Sensitivity
- Research authentic Japanese terminology
- Avoid cultural appropriation
- Respect motorsport heritage
- Consult community on technical accuracy
- Use kanji/katakana appropriately (not decoration)

## Product Categories

### Primary Products
1. **Apparel:** Performance tees, hoodies, hats
2. **Garage Decor:** Posters, flags, stickers
3. **Accessories:** Patches, keychains

## Example Descriptions

### On-Brand Design Example

**"Hakone Touge" Poster**
- Technical elevation map of Hakone mountain pass
- Topographic lines showing route
- Japanese kanji labels for key points
- Bold red/black/white color scheme
- Angular typography
- Clean vector illustration
- Authentic route representation

**AI Prompt Example:**
```
JDM drift car illustration, AE86 on mountain pass, dynamic angle 
showing tire smoke, bold red and black color scheme, Japanese kanji 
accents, isolated white background, t-shirt design, technical aesthetic
```

### Off-Brand Design Example

**What to Avoid:**
- Generic "JDM Life" text without context
- Copied manufacturer logos (copyright violation)
- Rising sun flag overuse (stereotypical)
- "Tokyo Drift" movie references (not authentic)
- Inaccurate car specifications
- Poorly researched Japanese text

## Brand Voice Characteristics

**Communication Style:**
- **Tone:** Passionate, knowledgeable, respectful
- **Language:** Technical but accessible, authentic
- **Approach:** Community-focused, heritage-conscious
- **Personality:** Enthusiast-driven, precision-oriented

## Quality Checklist

Before finalizing any design:
- [ ] Technically accurate (car specs, terminology)
- [ ] Culturally appropriate (Japanese elements)
- [ ] No copyrighted content
- [ ] Authentic JDM references (not generic)
- [ ] Dynamic composition (motion/speed implied)
- [ ] High contrast colors
- [ ] Respects motorsport heritage
- [ ] Community-appropriate language
- [ ] Print-safe bold colors
- [ ] Suitable for premium price point
